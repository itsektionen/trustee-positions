## President

### Description

Booking sports events that the people of the IT-chapter wants for the benefit of health and well being.

- **Access Privileges:** What areas the position has access to that the normal student doesn't.
- **Time Requirement:** 2-4h/ week.

### Experiences gained

A lot of communication skills such as reading people and matching demand. A lot about booking and billing etc.. You also learn a lot of new economics skills by budgeting and lay a foundation for the activities throughout the year…
