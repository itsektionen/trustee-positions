## vice President

### Description

As vice president of the business relations committee you step in for the president when they are absent and take over their responsibilities. You also handle the tasks that you and the president agree to delegate.

- **Access Privileges:** What areas the position has access to that the normal student doesn't.
- **Time Requirement:** Depends on what tasks the president delegates to you.

### Experiences gained

Managment, communication, networking, organization, CRM
