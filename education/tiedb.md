## Program Responsible Student (PAS) for TIEDB

### Description

You are responsible for the students’ educational influence in the TIEDB program.

Your tasks include arranging and taking part in course and program evaluations, as well as reviewing their results. You also arrange grade meetings for the students in the tiedb program. In this role you work to improve the parts of the program that need development and you are available for students’ opinions and questions regarding education.

- **Access Privileges:** What areas the position has access to that the normal student doesn't.
- **Time Requirement:** 20h /term

### Experiences gained

Huge insight into the studies and why they are structured the way they are.
