## Program Responsible Student (PAS) for MASTER

### Description

As program representative you are responsible for the students’ educational influence in the master’s programs at the school of EECS.

Your tasks include working to appoint a course committee for every master’s course, arranging and taking part in course and program evaluations, and reviewing their results. You also work to improve areas of the program that need development. You are available for students’ opinions and questions regarding education.

- **Access Privileges:** What areas the position has access to that the normal student doesn't.
- **Time Requirement:** 20h/ term.

### Experiences gained

Huge insight into the studies and why they are structured the way they are.
