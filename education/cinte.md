## Program Responsible Student (PAS) for CINTE

### Description

You are responsible for getting the students opinions on the studies to go through and reach KTH. Taking in opinons from the students and have meetings with professors.

- **Access Privileges:** What areas the position has access to that the normal student doesn't.
- **Time Requirement:** 20h/ term.

### Experiences gained

Huge insight into the studies and why they are structured the way they are.
