## Program Responsible Student (PAS) for TIDAB

### Description

- Going to study committee meetings (both InTernal and THS-wide ones)
- Going to programråd
- Gathering feedback from students about courses
- Forwarding feedback to professors
- Double-checking the schedule for upcoming years

---

- **Access Privileges:** What areas the position has access to that the normal student doesn't.
- **Time Requirement:** 20h/ term

### Experiences gained

How programs and courses are organized behind the scenes
