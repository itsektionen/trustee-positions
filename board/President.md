## Chapter President

### Description

As president you lead the work of the board and are responsible for the chapter’s ongoing activities. You make sure that everything in the chapter runs smoothly. You also call the board meetings and guide the work that the board does.

As president you are part of the THS Presidents Council, which means you attend their meetings and work together with the other presidents.

- **Access Privileges:** What areas the position has access to that the normal student doesn't.
- **Time Requirement:** 5-20h/week (Depending on the week)

### Experiences gained

Managements, writing emails, meetings, handling stressfull situations.
