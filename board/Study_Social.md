## Board member responsible for Study Social Activities

### Description

As the board member responsible for study social activities you work long-term to develop the student life within the chapter. You cooperate with the committees and groups that carry out this work in practice.

You coordinate the committees that are active in the chapter’s study social activities and act as the link between them and the board. You meet with the committee presidents to stay updated on their work and to bring their questions to the board, as well as return answers to them.

If no other person is better suited, you also attend the meetings arranged by THS Lokalråd.

- **Access Privileges:** What areas the position has access to that the normal student doesn't.
- **Time Requirement:** 10-20h/ month

### Experiences gained

Communication, bookings
