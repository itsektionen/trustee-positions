## Board member responsible for Communication

### Description

As board:

As a member of the board you perform all the board related tasks that are not handled by the precedium. In the coming year much of this will be move related. Board members are responsible for working with the board's strategic questions as well as the areas the board considers important. You will attend board meetings and do smaller tasks such as writing descriptions for trustee-positions, going to external events where you represent the chapter,cook food for the chapter meetings and cleaning days, etc. Towards the end of the year you are expected to help with the planning of the thankyou-party for all the trustees.

As head of committee:

Ensure the smooth operation of the communication committee, big areas are communication within the chapter (which is done together with secretary), externally with other chapters and student organizations, PR and merch for the chapter, Alumni contact, maintenance of communication channels and admin to the email system/accounts used within the chapter. Delegation here is important!

- **Access Privileges:** What areas the position has access to that the normal student doesn't.
- **Time Requirement:** 22-34h/month

### Experiences gained

Communication, delegation, management, team work, social skills.
