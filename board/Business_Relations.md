## Board member responsible for Business Relations

### Description

As the board member responsible for business relations you work to strengthen the cooperation between the chapter and the business sector.

As the head of the business relations committee you manage your committee members and hold frequent meetings to ensure good communication with the rest of the board. Furthermore you attend meetings arranged by THS Näringslivsråd.

- **Access Privileges:** What areas the position has access to that the normal student doesn't.
- **Time Requirement:** 10-30h/month

### Experiences gained

Managment, communication, networking, organization, CRM
