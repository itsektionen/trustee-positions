## Member

### Description

As election committee member its your job to motivate people to go for roles in the chapter as well as conduct interviews with potential candidates in preperation for chapter meetings.

- **Access Privileges:** What areas the position has access to that the normal student doesn't.
- **Time Requirement:** 20 hours/year

### Experiences gained

You get to meet a variety of people in the election committee, and you get to be a part of the democratic process
