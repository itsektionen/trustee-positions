## Convener

### Description

You plan and book the meetings for the election committee. The election committee is responsible for marketing, conducting interviews, and making recommendations for all major elections in the chapter.

- **Access Privileges:** What areas the position has access to that the normal student doesn't.
- **Time Requirement:** Varies greatly over the year, most time will be spent close to the election meeting.
