## vice President

### Description

As vice president of the JML committee you step in for the persident when they are absent and take over their responsibilities. You also work togther with the president to make the chapter safer and a better environment for everyone. You also handle the tasks that you and the president agree to delegate.

- **Access Privileges:** What areas the position has access to that the normal student doesn't.
- **Time Requirement:** 10h/ term

### Experiences gained

You gain a good understanding of who to contact in case of discrimination/safety in the student life and some public speaking experience during the reception
