## Stickkontaktsansvarig

### Description

It's the chapters Honorary Position. It's given to people that have been very active in the chapter and especially the board (not the masteries). Usually given to one person but has been given to two people before. On paper you should keep track of all the power plugs and outlets, but other than that you are not allowed to have any role within the chapter.

- **Access Privileges:** What areas the position has access to that the normal student doesn't.
- **Time Requirement:** 0 time or however much time you take on checking all the plugs.

### Experiences gained

Infinite
