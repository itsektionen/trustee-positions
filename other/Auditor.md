## Auditor

### Description

As Auditor you review the chapters accounting and management to make sure they follow swedish law, THS bylaws, and the IT chapter bylaws.

- **Access Privileges:** What areas the position has access to that the normal student doesn't.
- **Time Requirement:** A lot of time at the end of the year for the annual report. During the rest of the year you just look through SM and StyM protocols.

### Experiences gained

You learn a lot about the bylaws throughout the year. You also learn a lot through the annual report.
