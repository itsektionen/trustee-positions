## Skattmästare

### Description

On top of participating in the general work, ie meetings and planning work, you handle TMEIT's finances. Mainly keeping track of how much we spend for events and on which specific posts (food, alcohol and other (those are all the categories needed)). You also handle some group bookings. Should be noted that the budget for specific events such as pubs are generally not a concern for the SkattM as those events are to pay for themselves.

- **Access Privileges:** What areas the position has access to that the normal student doesn't.
- **Time Requirement:** 3-8h/ week.

### Experiences gained

Conducting meetings, long term planning, handling finances and to a certain degree HR/management skills.
