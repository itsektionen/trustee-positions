## Board Member (MUX)

### Description

MUX are the reception board members, but only some are trustee elected. As a MUX your role is decided together with INGEN and NÅGON and can therefore vary year to year. You might be in charge of logistics and bookings, fadders, pr, or anything else that might be required during reception.

- **Access Privileges:** What areas the position has access to that the normal student doesn't.
- **Time Requirement:** Heavily dependent on what your role is but around 6-10h/day during reception.

### Experiences gained

The experiences you gain vary wildly depending on what your role is decided to be.
